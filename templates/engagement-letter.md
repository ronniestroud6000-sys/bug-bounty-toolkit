# Engagement Letter — Short Form

> **Use this for smaller engagements** (<$3,000) where a full SOW is overkill but you still need authorization in writing. For larger or longer engagements, use `scope-of-work.md` instead.
>
> ⚠️ Not legal advice. Have a licensed attorney review before using for a real engagement.

---

**Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]

**To:** [Client Name], [Client Title], [Client Company]
**From:** [Your Name], [Your Company]

**Subject:** Engagement Letter — Security Assessment of [System]

---

[Client Name],

This letter confirms our agreement for a security assessment engagement as follows:

## Scope

- **In-scope:** [Specific URLs / assets]
- **Out-of-scope:** Anything not listed above, DoS testing, social engineering, physical security

## Timeline

- Engagement begins: [YYYY-MM-DD]
- Draft report: [YYYY-MM-DD]
- Final report: [YYYY-MM-DD]

## Testing Activities Authorized

You authorize me to perform the following against the in-scope assets listed above:

- Automated security scanning
- Manual HTTP-layer penetration testing
- Source code static analysis (if source provided)
- Content discovery and fuzzing within reasonable rate limits

I will NOT perform:
- Denial-of-service attacks
- Data exfiltration beyond proof-of-concept
- Social engineering of your employees
- Any activity not listed as authorized

## Deliverable

A professional PDF report with:
- Executive summary
- Prioritized findings with reproduction steps
- Remediation recommendations
- Debrief call up to 60 minutes

One retest of Critical/High findings is included within 30 days of report delivery.

## Fee

**$[X,XXX]**, billed 50% upfront ($[X,XXX]) and 50% on report delivery ($[X,XXX]).

## Confidentiality

I will treat all information obtained during this engagement as Confidential. I will securely delete your data within 60 days of engagement completion.

## Authorization & Indemnification

You confirm that you own or have authority to authorize testing of the in-scope assets listed above. You indemnify me against claims arising from testing performed within this authorization.

## My Liability

My total liability under this engagement is limited to the fees you pay.

---

To accept, please reply to this email with "I agree" (or sign and return a PDF version), along with:

- A technical point of contact I should coordinate with during testing
- Test account credentials if authenticated testing is in scope
- Any specific rate limits or blackout windows I should observe

Testing will begin after I receive your written acceptance and the upfront payment.

Looking forward to working together.

Best,
